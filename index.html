FAQ

Questions, answered.

What exactly is the free Brand Direction Suite?

+

Within 48 hours of filling out our intake form, you'll receive 2-3 curated brand directions. Each includes a color palette, typography pairing, and visual mood treatment — your business name rendered in each style so you can see what it could look like. It's completely free. If you love one, pick a package and we'll build it out. If you don't, walk away. No cost, no commitment, no hard feelings.

How are you so much cheaper than agencies?

+

No office. No team of 12. No fancy client dinners. No 45-minute "discovery calls." We've stripped out everything that makes agencies expensive but doesn't make your brand better. You're paying for the work — the actual logo, the actual brand system — not the experience of being a client.

What if I don't like the preview directions?

+

Then you walk away having paid nothing. The preview is free, no strings attached. We'd rather you see our work and decide it's not for you than pay for something you hate. That said, we're pretty good at this — most people find at least one direction they love.

How is this different from Fiverr?

+

Fiverr connects you with overseas freelancers using templates. You're usually getting a logo that 50 other businesses already have. We're U.S.-based, we understand American small businesses, and every brand we build is custom. Plus, you get to see the direction before you pay — no Fiverr seller offers that.

Do I own the final files?

+

100%. Once you pay, everything we create is yours. Full ownership, all file formats, use it however you want, forever. We don't do licensing fees or usage restrictions. Your brand is your brand.

Why no phone calls?

+

Because calls take time — yours and ours. Our intake form captures everything we need to build your brand. It's faster for you, cheaper for us, and the results are just as good. If something's unclear, we'll email you. If you absolutely need to talk, we can make exceptions — but most people prefer the speed and simplicity.

What if I need revisions?

+

Revisions are included in every package. Starter includes 3 rounds, Brand Kit and Full Identity include unlimited revisions until you're happy. We don't consider a project done until you love it.

How long until I get my brand?

+

After you approve a direction and pay, delivery times are: Starter (Logo Only) in 3-5 business days, Brand Kit in 5-7 business days, Full Identity in 7-10 business days. Rush delivery available if you need it faster — just ask.
